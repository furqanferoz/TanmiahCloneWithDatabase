A progressive investment partner

A progressive investment partner, and one of the largest employers in the Kingdom, Alturki supports Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 by facilitating economic diversification and supporting societal development. The Company is committed to the creation of value across all Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) topics and invests responsibly in order to grow its business while supporting the development of economies, improving lives, and preserving the planet for future generations.
